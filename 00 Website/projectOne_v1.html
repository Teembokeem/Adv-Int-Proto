ideation

The natural system I chose to emulate was the growth of a plant. I decided to choose a plant, with soil, weather, and time as factors that could be introduced into the system.

diagram

This flow diagram generalizes the key descriptors of the element, how other elements could interact with it, as well as how the player could interact/disrupt the system. The key concept is to programmatically define the system with incremental levels of complexity. Adding interactivity can be established once the system is coded in "equilibrium".

prototype

The prototype functions by first setting the environment. A coordinate grid, the "ground", is created by instancing a Soil class that randomly rolls a soil type. A seed of a random plant type is then sown into random plots of ground. Sprites from a free online resource were used as placeholders to help debug the runtime environment. Next steps are to introduce time and relative functions to start the developing process for the plant and soil.
